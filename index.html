NUTHAN ABHIRAM CHENELLI

           

Hi, I’m Nuthan Abhiram Chenelli — a Mining Engineer and GIS Analyst with a strong passion for sustainable resource management and environmental solutions. My journey spans from working in underground and open-pit mines to applying advanced geospatial analysis and LiDAR data for urban forestry and renewable energy projects. I’ve also explored sustainable agriculture through hands-on farm experience, giving me a unique perspective on balancing technology, environment, and community needs. Through this website, I’d like to share my work, projects, and experiences that reflect my skills and vision for building a more sustainable future.
